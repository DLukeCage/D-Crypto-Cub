The increased popularity and financial gain of cryptocurrencies have led to adopting these digital assets into daily routines and cultural practices. Whether with nfts in artwork or used as payment in stores. Crypto has massive implementation within our culture, not only with financial means but social aspects as well.

Crypto name Descripton culture aspect
NFT's NFT (Non-fungible Token) refers to digital assets with unique characteristics. Examples of NFT include crypto artwork, collectibles, game items, financial products, and more.
DEFI DeFi or Decentralized Finance refers to financial services that are built on top of distributed networks with no central intermediaries. Examples of DEFI include saving earning intrest accounts, lending, earning higher intrest than current banks offer.
SOCIAL NETWORK COINS Decentralized social network(SNC) refers to more user control over a centralized controlled social media Examples of SNC include any non-centralized social site similar to Facebook, Twitter, and Youtube, but decentralized.
