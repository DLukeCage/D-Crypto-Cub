Some information on the history of crypto currewncy

Modern cryptocurrencies were first mentioned in 1988. The concept fully emerged in 2009 with the release of a white paper that explains the foundations of blockchain and bitcoin. The author of the white paper is “Satoshi Nakamoto,” which is presumably a pseudonym for either a person or group of people.

BlockChain

"According to IBM, "Blockchain defined: Blockchain is a shared, immutable ledger that facilitates the process of recording transactions and tracking assets in a business network. An asset can be tangible (a house, car, cash, land) or intangible (intellectual property, patents, copyrights, branding). Virtually anything of value can be tracked and traded on a blockchain network, reducing risk and cutting costs for all involved"BLOCKCHAIN

Crypto-Market

The crypto markets come in cycles. Like in a traditional stock market, you have ups and downs or better called Bull & Bear markets. You basically have three primary cycles; Bitcoin bull run, alt season, And the bear market.CRYPTO CYCLES This video show you how the diffrent cycles work and when they occur.

  • The bitcoin bull-run usually last for about 9-10 months, during which the price of bitcoin gradully increase throughtout those 9-10 months.
  • Alt-saeson is when the bull runs is occuring and alt-coins(any cryptocurrency that is not bitcoin) rises in price during the bull run and a month or two after the bitcoin cycle ends.
  • Bear market is considerd the winter cold months when all coins are down and the prices dont move up for about 3-4 years long.
