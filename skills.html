Skills are required to gain confidence and expereince in cryptocurrencies!!

You must develop a good skill-set , in order to be succsful in the crypto world

  • Research is vital to your success. You must do your own research when it comes to crypto investing. Don't just trust one person's viewpoint. Watch multiple videos, read multi articles, before making a decision on which coin to invest in.
  • Learn the lingo. What is FUD? What is a bull market? What is getting rekt? These are all crypto vocabulary that you must know the meaning of and have a filly understanding of each new and old terminology.
  • It's imperative to learn about the utility behind the coin you are investing in. Look at the website of the coin and get a sense of precisely what their goals are for now and the future; here is an example of a coin's website;POLKADOT
  • Last but definitely not least, practice safe and secure technics when working on the internet. Whether on mobile or desktop, it's a critical fundamental practice to be safe and secure!!
